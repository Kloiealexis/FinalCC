Meet Christina

Christina's goal as an experienced mortgage loan officer is to explore the many financial options available to you, and to work together to find the best one.  Christina wants to facilitate homeownership in and around her community so that the dreams of individuals and families can be realized, from first homes to last.

She strives to be a resource and supporter of the Realtor community, not just on the transactions part of it all, but for anything her partners need to help them grow in their own business.

She encourages you to explore the loan options her company has to offer and as always, reach out anytime via phone or email.
